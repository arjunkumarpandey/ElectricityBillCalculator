About Bijli Bachao is an initiative that aims to help residential customers to reduce their electricity consumption thereby reducing the monthly outflow of money towards electricity bill. The benefits of the initiatives are many folds:

1) People save money on their electricity bills.
2) Reduced energy consumption reduces our increasing dependence on a depleting resource: coal.
3) Reduced energy consumption from urban areas can help reducing energy poverty in the rural areas.
